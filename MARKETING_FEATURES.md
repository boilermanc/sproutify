# Sproutify App - Marketing Feature Overview

## What is Sproutify?

**Sproutify** is the ultimate companion app for aeroponic tower gardeners. It combines AI-powered planning, community engagement, and comprehensive plant management to help growers succeed—from first-time gardeners to experienced enthusiasts.

---

## Key Marketing Features

### 1. Sage AI Planner (Flagship Feature)
- Personal AI gardening assistant that helps plan your tower
- Conversational interface—just tell Sage what you want to grow
- Get plant pairing recommendations and placement suggestions
- Save and reuse your favorite planting plans
- *"Like having a master gardener in your pocket"*

### 2. Comprehensive Plant Catalog
- 100+ plants with detailed growing information
- Community ratings and reviews from real growers
- Indoor and outdoor growing guides
- Quick-harvest plant recommendations for fast results
- Favorites system to save plants you love

### 3. Multi-Tower Support
- Works with **20+ tower brands**: Tower Garden, Lettuce Grow, Gardyn, Rise Gardens, Aerospring, AeroGarden, and many more
- Manage multiple towers from one app
- Custom port configurations (1-100 ports)
- Indoor/outdoor location tracking

### 4. Community Feed (Instagram-Style)
- Share your tower photos and harvests
- Before/after progress posts
- Like and comment on fellow growers' success
- Discover trending posts and featured gardens
- Filter by plant type, setup, and location

### 5. Gamification & Achievements
- Earn XP and level up as you grow
- Unlock badges for milestones and achievements
- Harvest Scorecard tracks your growing success
- Makes gardening fun and engaging

### 6. Pest & Disease Support
- Comprehensive pest identification guides
- Treatment recommendations (organic and conventional)
- Beneficial insects information
- Photo submission for expert help

### 7. Cost & Supply Tracking
- Track spending on seeds, nutrients, and supplies
- Budget management for your growing operation
- Supply inventory management
- Cost breakdown by category

### 8. Personalized Experience
- Set your gardening experience level
- Define growing goals (herbs, veggies, fruits, greens, flowers)
- Customized plant recommendations
- Progress tracking tailored to you
